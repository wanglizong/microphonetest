Frequently Asked Questions

How to test mouse buttons?

Click any button on your physical mouse. The corresponding part on the virtual mouse will light up blue if the signal is received correctly by your browser.

What is a good Polling Rate?

A higher Hertz (Hz) means the mouse reports its position more often. Standard mice are 125Hz, while gaming mice usually reach 500Hz or 1000Hz.

Does it support side buttons?

Yes, our tool detects Button 4 and Button 5 (Forward/Back). We automatically block default browser actions so you can test freely.

Is this test accurate?

Web-based tests are highly accurate for buttons and wheels. Polling rate results are smoothed to provide a stable reading despite browser latencies.
