How do I test my speakers?

Choose a test mode above. For stereo systems, use the Left/Right buttons. For general health, run the Frequency Sweep to check the full audible range (20Hz to 20,000Hz).

Why is there no stop button?

To provide a cleaner interface, selecting a new test automatically replaces the previous sound. You can also slide the Master Volume to 0% to silence the output.

Can this test damage my speakers?

At normal volume, no. However, be cautious with extremely high frequencies at 100% volume, as this can strain tweeters. Always start at 50% volume.
